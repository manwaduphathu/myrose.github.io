Long distance love

My rose, we've been apart, but still so near,
Visiting each month, wiping away each tear.
But now, a new job takes me far away,
Twelve long months, before I see your lovely face each day.
I promise to love you, no matter the distance wide,
You're forever in my heart, my love won't subside.
I know you're strong and free, with a heart so bright,
But I fear those who may try to steal your light.
Remember, 'Love Story' is ours, our own tale,
With a love so strong, it will never fail.
And when you're free, come visit me, anytime,
I'll be waiting, loving you, until the end of time.
As Taylor says, 'You Belong With Me', in my heart,
And I know, 'Everything Has Changed', but our love will never depart.
Bear with me, my love, and know I'll be true,
One day, all will be okay, and our love will shine through.
